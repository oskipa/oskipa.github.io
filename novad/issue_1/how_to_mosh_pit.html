How to: Mosh Pit

Play punk music

we recommend, Anarchy In the UK, Smells like Teen Spirit, and Big Red Car

Sway your head to the beat

Semi close your eyes. Feel the beat. Loosen the rest of your body, especially the arms. Gently hop up and down.

Let the energy of the music take over your body

As the energy rises, so does your hopping. Start moving in small circles. Wobble. You may bump into other people. That is ok.

Mosh

Once the chorus hits, so does your body. Bump into others. Shove them. As you do this, try staying on the beat.

Now you are a mosher!

Remember to make a circle to protect anyone who falls. Help them get back up. We are tryin to get bruises all in good fun
